Moer Renderer

Moer is a research-oriented, phycially-based renderer, providing an "easy to deploy and develop" rendering platform for Computer Graphics (CG) and Computer Vision (CV) research. It is developed by the NJUMeta group from Nanjing University.

Moer also has an educational version: Moer-lite, accompanied with several assignments (Assignment 0,  Assignment 1,   Assignment 2,  Final Assignment...).
